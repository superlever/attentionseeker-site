Frequently Asked Questions

You've got questions, we've got answers

How accurate are synthetic audience results?

Attention Seeker doesn’t predict the future — it provides directional insight based on simulated audience psychology.

It helps you identify stronger and weaker directions before launch. Real-world A/B testing still matters, but you start with better contenders instead of raw guesses.

Is this a replacement for real A/B testing?

No. It’s a pre-launch filter.

Instead of sending every idea into paid traffic, you narrow and refine first. Then you A/B test what’s already been pressure-tested — saving time, budget, and momentum.

How is this different from just asking ChatGPT for feedback?

ChatGPT and other LLMs gives general feedback.

Attention Seeker simulates defined audience profiles, compares variants side by side, ranks them, and provides structured insight — not just opinions.

It’s built for decision-making, not brainstorming. Depending on your testing configuration, you can lean on the wisdom of up to 128 AI agents per test.

Who is this best for?

It’s built for marketers, founders, agencies, and teams making high-stakes messaging decisions — especially before launch or before scaling paid traffic.

If you’re choosing between creative directions and want signal before spend, it’s for you.
